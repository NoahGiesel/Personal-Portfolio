Who am I ...

Hey. I'm Noah, a 22 years old self-taught and highly motivated Front-End developer! I'm from Germany and currently based in Italy!

After i graduated High School in mid 2016, i decided to study IT at Univeristy of Florence. I've learned and experienced so many new things in such a short period of time, like Algorithms and Data Structures, Concurrent Programming, Database Design, Java and much more!

In April 2018 i decided to learn Web Development, in particular Front-End. I learned HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript and currently learning React !

I learned really quick thanks to plenty of YouTube videos and Udemy courses such as "Modern React with Redux [2019 Update] ", "React - The Complete Guide" , "The Complete JavaScript Course 2019" , "JavaScript: Understanding the Weird Parts ".
